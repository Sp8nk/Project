Srimad Bhagavad Geeta

The sermon given by Lord Krishna to Arjun just before the start of the Mahabharata war is known as Shrimad Bhagwat Gita . It is a part of the Bhishma Parva of the Mahabharata . The Gita has 18 chapters and 700 verses. The Gita is counted among the Prasthanatrayi , which also includes the Upanishads and Brahmasutras . Therefore, according to the Indian tradition, the Gita has the same place as the Upanishads and Dharmasutras . The Upanishads have been called cow and the Gita its milk.
